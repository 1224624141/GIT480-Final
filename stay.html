Hotel Options

Enjoy exclusive discounts on nearby hotels when you book through our official partners. Don't miss out - secure your stay and get ready for a weekend of unforgettable music and fun!

Hilton Chicago

720 S Michigan Ave, Chicago, IL 60605•(312) 922-4400

Overlooking Grant Park, Lake Michigan, and Museum Campus, you'll be situated on Michigan Avenue, where incredible experiences are around every corner. Hilton Chicago has a pool, free wifi, AC, is pet friendly, and pay to park.

Book Hilton

Best Western Grant Park Hotel

1100 S Michigan Ave, Chicago, IL 60605•(312) 922-2900

Best Western is located just across the road from Grant Park. A functional hotel with suites that have views of Lake Michigan. Best Western has free breakfast, free wifi, free parking, AC, but is not pet friendly.

Book Best Western

Homewood Suites by Hilton Chicago Downtown South Loop

59 E 11th St, Chicago, IL 60605•(312) 753-3100

Located in a high-rise building with a glass facade, this modern all-suite hotel is a less than 10 minute walk from Grant Park. Homewood Suites has a pool, free wifi, AC, free breakfast, free parking, and is pet friendly.

Book Homewood Suites

Parking Garage

Millennium Garages is situated beneath Grant Park, Millennium Park & Maggie Daley Park which makes it the most convenient garage to park at when attending the festival. The four garages consist of Millennium Lakeside Garage, Millennium Park Garage, Grant Park South & Grant Park South.
